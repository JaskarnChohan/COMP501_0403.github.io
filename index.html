- AI Art uses cutting-edge machine learning technology to create artwork and images so advanced that it could pass off as being created by a human.


- It could fuel the rise of misinformation.


- The visual arts will be democratized, making it accessible even to people without formal artistic training.

- Discussions regard if the AI is expressing true creativity, who actually owns an AI generated artwork, and how this technology could potentially either fuel or stagnate artistic creativity.


- The rapid improvement in the quality of AI art is due to it being trained on millions of images from the internet, thus sparking discussion about copyright violations and if the technology is ethical.


- AI Art is an interesting and important topic that shows the role of AI in our society.
